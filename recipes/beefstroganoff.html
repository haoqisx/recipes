Beef Stroganoff

Description

First created in Tsarist Russia, beef stroganoff is a widely renowned dish, known for its delightfully aromatic sauce and its near-addictive egg noodles. Although not the most common dish, one taste is enough to convince anyone that beef stroganoff deserves a spot among the best dishes mankind has concocted. It originally gained popularity for being a rather simple meal to cook, but that the procedure is not exceedingly complex has no ties to its flavor; in fact, many argue that no overengineered, intricate mess of a dish can replicate the deep, rich, and uniquely homey palate of a good beef stroganoff.
