Ground Beef Burrito

Description

This dish is edible, as long as you do not completely mess up the cooking process. For most, that is enough.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of black beans, partially drained
  • 1 cup of salsa
  • 2/3 cup of water
  • 1 (1 ounce) package of taco seasoning
  • 4 (8 inch) flour tortillas, cut into strips
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) carton of sour cream
